The Engine

5 Core Mechanics

How we build the defensible decision asset.

01

Decision Objects

We don't sell "collaboration." We sell a structured data format. The Decision Object is the atomic unit of the system.

  • → Problem (Structured Input)
  • → Options (Generated Alternatives)
  • → Evidence (Linked Signal Graph)
  • → Tradeoffs (Forced Negative Assertion)
  • → Outcome (Back-propagated Truth)

This structure creates the training dataset for our future models.

02

Forced Friction

Competitors optimize for "ease of use." We optimize for "quality of thought." We intentionally introduce friction to prevent bad decisions.

⊘ BLOCKING: "You cannot approve until you accept a specific downside."

This creates liability. The person clicking "Approve" owns the risk.

03

The Truth Loop

Every decision is a prediction. We close the loop. We connect actual metrics back to the prediction, calculating the "Accuracy Score" of the organization.

04

Algorithmic Governance

We don't rely on human intuition alone. We score every decision *before* it's made based on signal density, bias patterns, and historical accuracy.
